Keeping Things Whole

In a field
   I am the absence
      of field.
         This is
           always the case.
             Wherever I am
               I am what is missing.
                When I walk
                 I part the air
                and always
               the air moves in
             to fill the spaces
           where my body's been.
         We all have reasons
      for moving.
   I move
to keep things whole.
